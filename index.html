SAINTS Lab

Sustainable Artificial Intelligence for Sciences. We are a research team in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Copenhagen, working at the intersection of AI and sustainability. Our work advances AI methods for sustainability applications across the sciences while also addressing the sustainability of AI itself. This includes developing novel algorithms that enhance the resource efficiency of AI and examining its broader environmental, social, and economic impacts.
