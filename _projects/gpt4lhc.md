### Project Overview

The Large Hadron Collider (LHC) is among the most powerful instruments in physics, but the massive amount of LHC data produced thus far (100,000+ TB) requires innovative analysis strategies. In this project, Generative Pre-Trained Transform-style models will be applied to the LHC data. This approach has shown success in natural language processing and could open a new era of unsupervised data analysis.
