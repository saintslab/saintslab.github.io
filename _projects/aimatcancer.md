### Project Overview

This project aims to develop AI to automate radiotherapy workflows, reducing workload, and thereby enabling personalized adaptation based on patient responses. We propose a novel framework for learning from large, well-regulated radiotherapy datasets, utilizing adaptable architectures capable of handling diverse tasks, settings, and populations.
