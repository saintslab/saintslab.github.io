### Project Overview
SustainML is an EU-funded project that stands for a new way of developing Machine Learning (ML) algorithms to reduce the carbon footprint at all stages of the ML application lifecycle, from the design and exploration phase to the final training of the production systems. It also covers a continuous online recycling phase during deployment for the inference process.
